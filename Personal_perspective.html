Why I choose Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering is the field that best aligns with my two favorite subjects: mathematics and physics. Beyond this, I am strongly motivated to explore how the electrical devices surrounding my daily life are designed and developed. In the long term, I aspire to create practical inventions that can be applied in real-world contexts. From a broader perspective, I dream of a future in which my products become commercially viable under my own management.
