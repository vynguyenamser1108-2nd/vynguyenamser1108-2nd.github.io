Overall introduction

My full name is Nguyen Le Phuong Vy. I am currently a student at Hanoi–Amsterdam High School for the Gifted, majoring in the mathematics specialized class.

I was born into a family living in the capital of Vietnam. My family consists of five members, and they have always been my greatest source of emotional support, helping me overcome challenges throughout my life.

Growing up in Northern Vietnam, I have sometimes encountered gender stereotypes that underestimate girls’ potential. Choosing to apply for Electrical Engineering is not only a way for me to pursue my passion, but also an effort to challenge outdated perceptions that prevent many capable individuals from reaching their full potential.
